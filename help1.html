Frequently Asked Questions

What is the LMS used for?

The LMS is used for managing and delivering online courses and educational content, allowing students and instructors to interact, submit assignments, and track progress.

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on 'Forgot Password'. Follow the instructions to reset your password via email.

How can I contact support?

You can contact support by submitting a request through the 'Feedback' form or by emailing support@example.com.
